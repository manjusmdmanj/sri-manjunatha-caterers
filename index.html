About Us

Your Trusted Catering Partner

15+ Years of Pure Vegetarian Excellence

At Sri Manjunatha Caterings, we bring over 15 years of culinary expertise and passion to every event. As a pure vegetarian catering service, we are committed to quality, freshness, and exceptional service, making us a trusted name in the catering industry.

We specialize in three distinct cuisines - South Indian, North Indian, and Chinese - creating customized menus that reflect your taste and preferences. Whether it's traditional dosas and sambar, rich North Indian curries, or flavorful Chinese dishes, we ensure your special day is remembered for the exceptional vegetarian food experience.
