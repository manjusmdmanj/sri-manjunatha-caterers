## How to Set Up Your Business Email

### Option 1: Google Workspace (Recommended - Professional)
**Cost:** ~₹125/month per email address

**Steps:**
1. Visit: https://workspace.google.com
2. Choose "Get Started" → Select "Business"
3. Enter your domain: `srimanjunathacaterers.com`
4. Verify domain ownership
5. Create email addresses (info@, orders@, etc.)
6. Set up email on your phone/computer

**Benefits:**
- Professional email with your domain
- 30GB storage per user
- Gmail interface
- Works on all devices
- Calendar, Drive, Meet included

### Option 2: Zoho Mail (Free Option)
**Cost:** FREE for up to 5 users

**Steps:**
1. Visit: https://www.zoho.com/mail/
2. Sign up for free
3. Add your domain
4. Verify domain
5. Create email addresses

**Benefits:**
- Completely free
- 5GB storage per user
- Professional email
- Mobile apps available

### Option 3: Microsoft 365 Business Basic
**Cost:** ~₹125/month per user

**Steps:**
1. Visit: https://www.microsoft.com/en-in/microsoft-365
2. Choose Business Basic plan
3. Add your domain
4. Create email addresses

**Benefits:**
- Professional Outlook email
- 50GB mailbox
- Office apps online
- Teams included

## Quick Setup Steps (Using Zoho - Free)

1. **Get a Domain First** (if you don't have one):
   - Buy domain from: Namecheap, GoDaddy, or Hostinger
   - Cost: ~₹500-1000/year for .com domain

2. **Set Up Zoho Mail (Free)**:
   ```
   1. Go to zoho.com/mail
   2. Click "Sign Up Now"
   3. Choose "Business Email"
   4. Enter your domain: srimanjunathacaterers.com
   5. Verify domain ownership (they'll give you DNS records)
   6. Add DNS records to your domain registrar
   7. Wait for verification (usually 24-48 hours)
   8. Create email: info@srimanjunathacaterers.com
   ```

3. **Access Your Email**:
   - Web: mail.zoho.com
   - Mobile: Download Zoho Mail app
   - Desktop: Use any email client (Outlook, Thunderbird)

## DNS Records Needed

When setting up email, you'll need to add these DNS records to your domain:

### MX Records (for receiving emails):
```
Priority: 10
Host: mx.zoho.com
```

### TXT Records (for verification):
```
SPF Record: v=spf1 include:zoho.com ~all
```

Your email provider will give you exact records to add.
